How it works

We combine three signals to form a verdict:

  • Provenance: Check for Content Credentials (C2PA) and other capture/edit markers.
  • Forensics: Compression patterns, metadata & model signals.
  • Heuristics: Visual cues (repeating textures, odd artifacts).

Outputs are: AI, Real, or Inconclusive with a confidence % and a one‑line reason.

What “Inconclusive” means

Signals sometimes conflict or quality is too low. In those cases we won’t guess.

For a stronger read: send the original file (not a screenshot) or a higher‑resolution version.

Appeal

Think we got it wrong? Reply to your receipt and attach the original file. We’ll re-check and update the verdict if warranted.

We review appeals within a reasonable time window; not all outcomes change.
